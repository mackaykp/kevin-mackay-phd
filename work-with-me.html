Work With Me

How I help teams

I help teams move from uncertainty to clarity by bringing structure, rigor, and momentum to analytical and research work. If you are facing complex data, unclear questions, or stalled projects, I can help you move forward with confidence.

I work with population, demographic, and administrative data across the full lifecycle - from problem definition and data quality through analysis, communication, and workflow design.

Who I Collaborate With

Audience

  • Public-sector teams navigating complex administrative or population data and needing analytical capacity
  • Organizations seeking clarity on data quality, governance, documentation, or reproducible workflows
  • Policy, program, and research teams that need rigorous analytical support without adding permanent headcount
  • Leaders who want actionable insight and decision-ready synthesis

What it’s like

  • Clear expectations and transparent communication at each step
  • Rigorous methods with practical, decision-ready outcomes
  • Strong documentation and reproducible workflows
  • Collaborative, stakeholder-aligned engagement
  • Ethical, governance-minded practice

How I can help

Full-lifecycle analytical support

I work across the entire analytics and research lifecycle - from requirements gathering and data validation to analysis, interpretation, and communication. I help teams move from uncertainty to clarity with solutions that are reliable, transparent, and maintainable.

Problem definition & scoping

I help teams clarify questions, identify constraints, and translate ideas into actionable analytical plans. That includes understanding stakeholder needs, assessing feasibility, and outlining the steps required to deliver meaningful results.

Data quality, governance & workflow design

I focus on validating data, documenting assumptions, and building reproducible workflows - systems that are trustworthy and aligned with governance expectations.

Analysis, research & insight generation

I conduct spatial, statistical, and mixed-methods analysis to support program monitoring, performance measurement, and policy-oriented decision-making, and translate findings for technical and non-technical audiences.

Communication, training & enablement

I develop dashboards, reports, presentations, and user guides so teams can understand and use their data - and I deliver training and workshops to support adoption of tools and good practice.

Credibility and fit

Credibility at a glance

  • Trusted by municipal, federal, and academic teams
  • Experience supporting policy, program, and research environments
  • Work applied to program monitoring, performance measurement, and decision support

What I don’t do

  • Purely software engineering or app-building engagements where analytics and research are not the core need
  • Long-term full-time contracts (I am open to part-time, project, and advisory arrangements)
  • Projects where data access, ethics, or governance foundations are not sufficiently clear to do responsible work
